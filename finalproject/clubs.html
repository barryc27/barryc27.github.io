Clubs:

MockTrial:

I have participated in Mock Trial for two years now. To be a member of Mock Trial you have to audition. The auditions are very selective and competitive. Last year I was a juror and this year I was a witness. I had an extremely fun time being a witness. My responsibilities include going to long meetings after school multiple times a week, memorizing my testimony and practicing my part! Because of our hard work, this year and past we made it to the County Finals. This was extremely exciting and stressful. I look forward to participating in Mock Trial again next year, and hopefully this time as a lawyer!

Science League:

Science League is a club where the members take around 4 tests a year after school on the current science they are taking in school. Last year I took the Biology tests as I took Honors Biology as a freshman and this year I took the Chemistry tests because I was in Honors Chemistry. To become a member you have to take a qualifying test and be in the top 4 for your subject. Science League was certainly a lot of fun and I look forward to participating in it again next year!
